Trust Snapshot (Diagnostic)

This quick diagnostic measures trust behaviors in three areas: Context (predictability), Mutual Relationship (goodwill), and Personality (willingness to be vulnerable / psychological safety).

18 items • 5-point scale • Instant feedback

Answer honestly based on your consistent behavior under pressure, not your intention.

Questions

1 = Strongly Disagree … 5 = Strongly Agree
